the things that irks me



People who assume they can borrow without asking.


Not closing doors or cabinets after use.


Not respecting personal space.


Misplacing items and then blaming others.


Talking loudly on the phone in quiet places.


Slow walkers in crowded areas.



Cutting in line without waiting your turn.


People who interrupt during conversations.


Loud chewing or slurping while eating.


Leaving dirty dishes in the sink.


Not covering the mouth when coughing or sneezing.
